About Me

My name is Tahaylia. I have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Foreign Languages, Spanish and German, respectively and a background in Software Engineering (Web design, Front-End & Back-End). I also have a strong interest in UI/UX Design.


I first got bitten by the 'coding bug' when I graduated from the University of Illinois at Chicago as a Modern Languages major and was looking to quench my ‘creative thirst’ while job hunting. I chose to occupy my time with photo editing, UI and logo design via Adobe Photoshop and Figma, as well as video editing with Adobe Premiere Pro. These creative undertakings quickly reminded me of how much I enjoyed personalizing a blog I once had with snippets of html code. I then decided to fully immerse myself into the wonderful world of scripting/programming languages via self-study, and, needless to say—I was hooked!

The joy I feel from designing and developing web pages has influenced my decision to switch my career path to the Tech industry. This decision led me to Arizona State University (ASU). However, being an individual with an avid thirst for knowledge in my prospective career, I recognized that the degree program I was pursuing at ASU (Full-Stack Web Development) did not bridge the 'gap' between school learning materials and real world job training skills. I've therefore, sought after enrolling in Per Scholas, a Software Engineering Program to refine my skills.


During my time at Per Scholas, something amazing happened! I've discovered that my creative hobbies (those mentioned previously) have only grown stronger with each project I designed and developed. I, therefore, have an overwhelming desire to add UI/UX design to my tech skills and contribute greatly to the field of technology.

If you'd like to know more about me or my projects, please send me an email. I would love to hear from you.
